Welcome to Bentley Cricket Club, The Home of the Mighty Blues!   You are here: Home » HOME

From small beginnings in 1953, the Bentley Cricket Club has gradually progressed to a large club. We now field 5 senior teams in the West Australian Suburban Turf Cricket Association (WASTCA)'s Saturday competition.

In 2013 the Bentley Blues celebrated our 60th anniversary, and we did it in style, winning the 1st Grade title for the second time, and the CaptureSport T20 competition.

Apart from our 1st Grade successes in 2009, & 2013, we have won 26 other premierships throughout our other grades, the latest two being the 2nd Grade, & One Day Competition, in 2015.

The Club trains on Tuesday & Thursday at Wyong Reserve from 5 - 7pm and new members are most welcome.
